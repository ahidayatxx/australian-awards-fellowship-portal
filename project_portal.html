Program Summary

Objective: Implement value-based digital health transformation in Indonesia through intensive 15-week fellowship program building Australia-Indonesia knowledge exchange capacity.

Timeline: November 8, 2025 - February 21, 2026 (15 weeks, 59 working days)

Budget: AUD $444,649 ($253,585 DFAT funded)

Participants: 9 fellows (5 government from TWG SATUSEHAT, 4 academic from eastern Indonesia universities)
